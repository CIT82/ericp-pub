Dio Brando!

This is my favorite character of all of JoJo Bizarre Adventur!

I love the way Araki Hirohiko draws him in the series and the references he does with him. There are so many iconic lines he says in the anime series, the voice actor Takehito Koyasu does an amazing job!

He is the main antagonist in the anime and manga series. He is the primary antagonist in Part 1 (Phantom Blood) and Part 3 (Stardust Crusaders). His ideals live on until part 6 (Stone Ocean) of the series. He is known for his cunning, charisma, and an instiable thirst for power! His legacy lives on throughout JoJo Bizarre Adventure series, making him one of the most memorable characters!

His stand, THE WORLD!
