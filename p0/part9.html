Jodio Joestar

He was diagnosed as possibly having antisocial personality disorder. A test revealed with several traits such as impulsiveness and an inablity to feel remorse. He is very reserved and solitary boy. His greatest ambition is to become rich.

Plot

This story takes place in Hawaii and plot of the story is how he will become rich! His journey to become filthy rich through the means of a Lava Rock with the power to draw valuable things to itself.
