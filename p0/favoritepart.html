Diamond is Unbreakable

By far my favorite part!

Josuke Higashikata is the best out of all JoJo's based on his personality. He's both a silly kid and cares for his friends greatly. I feel his stand ability able to heal others came from his good nature of caring for others first because his own stand couldn't do the same.

Super Strength: Crazy Diamond possesses immense physical strength!

Super Speed: Josuke's stand strikes exceed 300km/h (190 mph) and are able to catch a bullet fired in close proximity.

Crazy Diamond is a powerful stand for close-quarters combat.

The unconventional power of repairing broken items makes it a redoubtable and unpredictable Stand in a fight, but also an invaluable fighting partner as it can heal any wound!
